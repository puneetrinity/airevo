Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our AI product development services

How long does it take to build an AI MVP?

We deliver production-ready MVPs in 4-8 weeks, depending on complexity. This includes data preparation, model training/fine-tuning, API development, and deployment. Simple LLM integrations can be done in 2-3 weeks, while complex knowledge graph systems may take 6-8 weeks.

What tech stack do you use for AI search systems?

Our typical stack includes FastAPI for APIs, PostgreSQL with pgvector for vector storage, Redis for caching, and LangGraph for orchestration. We use HNSW indexing for fast similarity search and product quantization (PQ) for memory efficiency. For local deployments, we integrate Ollama.

Can you fine-tune open-source LLMs for our specific use case?

Yes. We specialize in fine-tuning open models like Llama, Qwen, and Mistral using Unsloth and Transformers. We handle the complete pipeline: data preparation, training on GPUs, quantization (4-bit/8-bit), and deployment via vLLM on RunPod, Hugging Face, or your infrastructure. Typical timeline is 2-4 weeks.

What makes your recruitment AI different?

We're former recruiters who built AI tools to solve our own pain points. Our systems include bias detection, ESCO skills taxonomy mapping, intelligent resume parsing, and job-candidate matching using semantic embeddings. We understand both the technical and human aspects of hiring.
